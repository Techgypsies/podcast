Episode 12: Snake Oil


Welcome to the twelfth episode of the Tech Gypsies podcast, Kin Lane and Audrey Watters' weekly discussion of the latest technology (and political) news.

In this episode, we talk about the history of technology (and the history of marketing technology), technology and an obsession with "real time" data, and Hillary Clinton's "innovation agenda."

Here are the articles we referenced:

  • "The Moral Economy of Tech" by Maciej Ceglowski
  • "Why I Am Suing the Government" by Christian Sandvig
  • "(Marketing) Virtual Reality in Education: A History" on hackeducation.com
  • "Clinton's Tech Policy Targets Young Entrepreneurs" in The New York TImes
