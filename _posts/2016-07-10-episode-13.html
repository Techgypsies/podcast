Episode 13: Drones


Welcome to the thirteenth episode of the Tech Gypsies podcast, Kin Lane and Audrey Watters' weekly discussion of the latest technology (and political) news.

In this episode, we talk about Isaac Asimov's "First Law of Robotics," the FAA's new rules on drones, the militarization of technology, and killer robots.
